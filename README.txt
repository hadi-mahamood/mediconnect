MediConnect Kerala — interactive front-end prototype

Open index.html in a modern browser. No installation or build step is required.

Restricted workspace demo access code: MEDI2026

Included flows:
- Four switchable workspaces: Patient, Doctor, Admin, and Support & Compliance
- Responsive patient dashboard
- Verified-doctor browsing and filtering
- Consent-gated consultation booking
- Appointments and simulated waiting room
- Health-record privacy and audit log
- Kerala-focused legal and compliance centre
- Emergency 112 escalation
- Malayalam interface placeholder
- Doctor queue, consent-scoped charts, schedule, prescription safety, and earnings
- Admin doctor verification, users, payments, operations, and analytics
- Grievance cases, privacy requests, incident response, and audit explorer

Important: This prototype is not medical or legal advice. Before production,
have Kerala-qualified legal counsel review the full workflow and connect it to
real registration, identity, consent, grievance, security, payment, pharmacy,
prescription, and emergency systems.
